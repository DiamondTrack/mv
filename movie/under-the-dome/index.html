Pilih Season dulu Kak

Season 1 (2013)

Isolation and Adaptation

The residents of Chester's Mill suddenly find themselves trapped under a mysterious transparent dome...

Season 2 (2014)

Power and Control

Tensions rise as the residents begin to vie for power and control over the town. Internal and external conflicts intensify, and some characters start to reveal their darker sides. The mystery of the dome deepens with the emergence of strange phenomena and new threats.

Season 3 (2015)

Truth and Liberation

The residents of Chester's Mill start to uncover the truth behind the dome and the forces controlling it. They face the final challenge of freeing themselves from the dome, with many sacrifices along the way. This season focuses on uncovering secrets and resolving the conflicts that have been brewing since the beginning.
